HackemUp: A Hacker News bookmarklet

Keep track of what's changed on HackerNews since the last time you looked.

Bookmarklet: HackemUp - A HN Bookmarklet

Highlights and updates new articles, articles that are rising or falling fast (drop more than 3), as well as updating comment counts, points, times, and your karma.

Directions

  1. Open Hacker News.
  2. Run the bookmarklet above on the HN page (in Chrome, just drag the link onto the tab - for others, drag the link to the bookmark bar, then switch to NH, then click the bookmark link.)
  3. Read HN.
  4. Every couple of minutes, the page will magically refresh, with changes displayed.
  5. If you move to another tab, but leave HN open, no updates will happen until you get back.
